About Us

Established as a Partnership firm in the year 2016, we “Vidigo Power Solutions” are a leading Manufacturer and Trader of a wide range of Color Velvet Powder, Pencil Making And Coating Machine, etc. Situated in Ahmedabad (Gujarat, India), we have constructed a wide and well functional infrastructural unit that plays an important role in the growth of our company. We offer these products at reasonable rates and deliver these within the promised time-frame. Under the headship of our mentor “Mr. Vishal Sakhiya”, we have gained a huge clientele across the nation.

Products and Services offered

Pencil is a general necessity everywhere, from homes, schools to offices. In the recent times, it was only quality that customers used to consider while purchasing pencils. But now, in the modern times, customers do not only consider quality but also design. Helping pencil manufacturers in manufacturing quality and different in design pencils is our company, Vidigopencils.As a Partnership Firm, which is registered under the Indian Partnership Act 1932, we have been running since 2016. Our company is impressively performing the roles of a manufacturer and supplier by investing a large sum of money in improving production and delivery operations. We have been working in this industry to not only provide pencil making complete solution that includes Pencil Making Machine and Velvet Powder, but also Eraser, Pencils.
